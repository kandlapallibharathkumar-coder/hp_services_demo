Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about your Dell device repair? Find answers to commonly asked questions below.

Is this a Dell Authorized Service Center?

Yes, we are a certified, professional service provider for all Dell laptops, desktops, and printers in Hebbal. We perform repairs in compliance with Dell quality standards.

Where is the service center located in Hebbal?

We are situated at 123 Service Road, Hebbal, Bengaluru, Karnataka – 560024. For interactive map and landmarks, please visit our Location Page.

What are your working hours?

Our center is open Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. We are closed on Saturdays and Sundays.

Do you use original Dell spare parts?

Absolutely. We only use 100% genuine Dell parts for all screen replacements, battery fittings, motherboards, and keyboards. All replaced parts carry an official warranty.

How long does a laptop screen replacement take?

Screen replacements typically take 1 to 2 hours if parts are in stock. If it requires a specific screen trim, it might take up to 24 hours.

Do you charge for diagnostics?

Diagnostic checks are free if you choose to proceed with the repair. If you decide not to proceed with the service, a nominal testing fee of ₹250 applies.

Can you recover data from a crashed hard drive?

Yes, we have specialized lab tools to safely recover and backup data from corrupted, formatted, or logic-board failed HDDs and SSDs.

Do you provide onsite printer service?

Yes, we offer door-to-door diagnostic and repair support for commercial office printers and networks in the Hebbal area. Contact us to schedule an onsite engineer visit.

Is there a warranty on repairs?

Yes, all our services carry a 90-day warranty on labor. Replaced hardware components like batteries and screens come with a 6-month to 1-year manufacturer warranty.

How can I check the status of my repair?

You can track your service status by calling us directly at +91 80 4567 8901 or sending an email to support@dellhebbal.com along with your unique Job Card ID.
