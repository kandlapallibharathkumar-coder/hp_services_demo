Who We Are

Trusted Dell Servicing Since 2011

Dell Authorized Service Center Hebbal was established with one core mission: to provide certified, reliable, and premium-quality repairs for Dell systems in Bengaluru. Over the past 15 years, we have grown into North Bengaluru's most trusted repair laboratory, handling commercial fleet setups and private devices with equal precision.

Every device brought to our lab is diagnosed using official Dell diagnostics (like Dell SupportAssist) and serviced using original OEM replacement parts. Our technicians hold standard certifications and practice advanced micro-soldering procedures.

Our Journey

Milestones of Progress

Trace the history of our service center from a single repair desk in 2011 to Hebbal's premium lab.

2011

Service Center Inception

Opened as a standalone electronics repair store catering to desktop diagnostics and configurations in Hebbal.

2015

Authorized Dell Service Setup

Secured certified authorization to repair Dell systems and commercial desktop ranges, training engineers at official facilities.

2019

Clean Room Lab Expansion

Introduced BGA rework equipment and anti-static clean areas to handle micro-soldering motherboard chips and chip-level debugging.

2023

Corporate Fleet SLA Servicing

Partnered with multiple tech startups and offices along Hebbal's Outer Ring Road to provide priority AMC fleet protection contracts.

2026

Digital Booking & Same-Day Services

Launched live queue reservations and same-day delivery schedules for screen, keyboard, and storage upgrades.
